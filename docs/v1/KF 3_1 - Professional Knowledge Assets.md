Chapter 1 — Introduction

Professional Knowledge Assets (PKAs) are the primary products manufactured by the Knowledge Factory Platform.

They represent curated, governed, versioned, and commercially distributable collections of Knowledge Objects designed to deliver trusted expertise within a specific professional domain.

Unlike traditional documents or AI-generated responses, Professional Knowledge Assets combine structured knowledge, expert validation, workflows, reasoning rules, and supporting resources into reusable knowledge products.

Every Professional Knowledge Asset is built upon the Knowledge Object Model and governed by the Knowledge Governance Framework.

Chapter 15 — Architectural Impact

Professional Knowledge Assets are the primary commercial products of the Knowledge Factory Platform.

They transform governed Knowledge Objects into reusable, certifiable, and distributable knowledge products capable of supporting professionals, organisations, AI Workers, and enterprise applications.

By combining structured knowledge, workflows, rules, expert validation, governance, and lifecycle management into cohesive packages, Professional Knowledge Assets establish a new category of digital product: professionally governed, continuously evolving knowledge.

Professional Knowledge Assets therefore represent the tangible outcome of the Knowledge Factory vision, converting institutional expertise into enduring intellectual property that can be deployed, licensed, shared, and continuously improved across industries and professional domains.
