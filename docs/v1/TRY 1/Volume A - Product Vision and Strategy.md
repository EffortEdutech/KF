# Knowledge Factory

## Volume A - Product Vision and Strategy

**Version:** 1.0 (Architecture Baseline)

---

# 1. Introduction

Knowledge Factory is a professional knowledge manufacturing platform.

Its purpose is to capture expert knowledge, structure it into reusable knowledge assets, govern its quality, and package it into deployable Professional Knowledge Assets that can power AI-assisted work environments.

Knowledge Factory is a platform and architecture for turning organizational knowledge into usable, governed, executable knowledge products.

---

# 2. Vision

To become the trusted platform for transforming expert knowledge into governed, reusable, AI-ready professional knowledge assets.

Knowledge Factory should help organizations preserve institutional knowledge, reduce dependence on individual experts, and make high-quality guidance available at scale.

---

# 3. Mission

Knowledge Factory exists to manufacture professional knowledge.

It converts:

- Expert experience.
- Standards.
- Procedures.
- Templates.
- Project records.
- Lessons learned.
- Domain ontologies.
- Decision rules.
- Workflows.

into structured knowledge assets that can be searched, reasoned over, validated, packaged, installed, and executed inside compatible runtime platforms such as LADOS.

---

# 4. Core Product Idea

The core product idea is:

**Organizations do not only need AI chat. They need their own expert knowledge made available 24/7, under their own control.**

Generic AI can answer general questions. Knowledge Factory creates the governed knowledge layer that allows AI systems to answer using the organization's actual standards, procedures, terminology, and professional judgment patterns.

---

# 5. Target Customers

Knowledge Factory Version 1 is designed for organizations that depend on professional expertise, including:

- Engineering firms.
- Construction organizations.
- Government agencies.
- Regulated industries.
- Legal and compliance teams.
- Healthcare and technical operations teams.
- Training and knowledge management departments.
- Enterprises with large internal procedure libraries.

---

# 6. Business Outcome

The customer is not buying a technical platform alone.

The customer is buying:

**The organization's expert knowledge, available 24/7, with AI assistance, under full organizational control.**

The practical outcomes are:

- Preserve institutional knowledge.
- Reduce expert bottlenecks.
- Accelerate onboarding.
- Improve decision consistency.
- Make internal knowledge easier to use.
- Support private or local AI deployments.
- Reduce long-term knowledge access cost.
- Create reusable professional knowledge products.

---

# 7. Product Philosophy

Knowledge Factory is guided by these principles:

## Knowledge Before Automation

Automation is only valuable when it is grounded in trusted knowledge.

## Governance Before Scale

Knowledge should be validated, versioned, and approved before being distributed widely.

## Experts Remain Central

The platform helps experts preserve and scale their knowledge. It does not remove professional accountability.

## AI Uses Knowledge, It Does Not Own Knowledge

AI models provide reasoning and language capabilities. The organization's governed knowledge remains the source of truth.

## Assets Over Documents

The platform turns scattered documents into structured, reusable knowledge assets.

## Local Control Matters

Organizations should be able to run knowledge workflows using local infrastructure and local AI models where privacy, cost, or governance requires it.

---

# 8. Core Innovation

Knowledge Factory introduces the concept of a **Professional Knowledge Asset (PKA)**.

A PKA is an installable package of professional knowledge that may contain:

- Domain ontology.
- Knowledge graph.
- Standards and procedures.
- Templates.
- Reasoning rules.
- Workflows.
- AI prompts.
- Quality checklists.
- Calculation rules.
- Evidence references.
- User-facing assistant configuration.

When installed into a compatible runtime such as LADOS, a PKA becomes a knowledge-powered professional workspace.

---

# 9. Version 1 Scope Principle

Knowledge Factory Version 1 should focus on manufacturing and governing PKAs.

It should not attempt to become every possible runtime, marketplace, enterprise suite, or full workflow automation environment in the first version.

Version 1 must prove that:

1. Expert knowledge can be captured.
2. Knowledge can be structured into objects and graphs.
3. Knowledge can be governed and versioned.
4. A PKA can be built.
5. A PKA can be exported or installed into a runtime environment.
6. AI can answer using the governed knowledge asset.

---

# 10. Architecture Freeze

The following principles are frozen for Knowledge Factory Version 1:

- Knowledge Factory manufactures Professional Knowledge Assets.
- Knowledge Factory is described as a knowledge manufacturing platform, not a computer OS.
- Knowledge Objects are the core unit of structured knowledge.
- The Knowledge Manufacturing Pipeline is the core process.
- The Intelligence Engine assists extraction, classification, reasoning, retrieval, and validation.
- Knowledge Factory Studio is the primary user workspace.
- Knowledge Governance is mandatory.
- LADOS is treated as a compatible runtime platform for executing PKAs.
- Local development and local AI using Ollama must be supported.
